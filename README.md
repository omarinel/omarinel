# Hi there! Welcome to O'Marinel Development Hub 👋

This is the central development profile for **O'Marinel** — an ecosystem powering our premium cake delivery e-commerce platform and business infrastructure. We are building a modern, fast, and scalable suite of products to deliver the best user and operational experience.

## 🍰 Our Ecosystem (What We Build)

We manage and develop four core products that keep our business running smoothly:
* **Storefront** — Our high-performance, SEO-optimized customer-facing online shop.
* **CMS (Content Management System)** — The core digital asset and catalog management platform.
* **CRM (Customer Relationship Management)** — Internal system for order processing, logistics, and customer care.
* **LMS (Learning Management System)** — Internal training platform for our bakers, decorators, and staff.

## 💻 Tech Stack

We use a modern, robust, and highly efficient stack to build our products:

* **Frontend:** SvelteKit
* **Backend / Headless CMS:** Strapi
* **Database:** PostgreSQL
* **Containerization:** Docker
* **Deployment & Orchestration:** Coolify
